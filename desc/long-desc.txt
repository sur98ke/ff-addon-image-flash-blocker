Just place addon icon into any toolbal and click it. Then choose, which elements should be loaded and displayed:

"Images on, Flash on"
or
"Images off, Flash on"
or
"Images on, Flash off"
or
"Images off, Flash off"

Changes are applied immideately to all opened tabs, so you can use this addon to rapidly hide images and flash objects.

This add-on is able to block java embedded photos.
